DIVINE LITURGY HTML GENERATOR v0.0.0 (Testing Alpha)

Thank you for visiting the Divine Liturgy HTML Generator by John Marion. The intent of this site is to allow you to input certain local variables, and then produce the correct text of the Divine Liturgy of St John Chrysostom for any given day. An option to export to the PDF format is in the works.

HOW TO USE

The Settings page allows you to change information specific to your parish, turn certain optional portiond on or off, and list names of people for inclusion in the output. Once you have made changes, you must hit 'Apply' in order for you changes to be reflected in the final output. Settings will be saved in your browser. On this page, select a date and hit 'HTML page' to view the output as a webpage or 'PDF' to get a downloadable PDF.
